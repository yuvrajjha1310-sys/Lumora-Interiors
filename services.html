Good to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

01How long does a typical project take?

A single room typically takes 4–6 weeks from design approval to final styling, while full home projects range from 3–6 months depending on scope.

02Do you work outside Delhi?

Yes — while our studio is based in New Delhi, we take on select projects across the region. Get in touch and we'll confirm availability for your location.

03Can I hire you for just a consultation?

Absolutely. Our Essential package is designed for clients who want expert design direction without a full-service engagement.

04Do you offer furniture sourcing only?

Yes, we offer furniture sourcing and custom design as a standalone service for clients who already have a design plan in place.
