Our Story

Where Vision Meets Craftsmanship.

Lumora Interiors was founded with a simple belief: the spaces around us have the power to shape how we live, feel, and connect.

What began as a passion for refined interiors has evolved into a design studio dedicated to creating beautifully considered spaces for modern living.

From private residences to sophisticated commercial environments, every project is approached with curiosity, precision, and an appreciation for timeless design.
